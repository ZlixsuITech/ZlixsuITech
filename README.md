## 👨‍💻 About Me

<img align="right" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/212748842-9fcbad5b-6173-4175-8a61-521f3dbb7514.gif" width="240" alt="Engineer GIF"/>

I'm **Fadli**, an Electrical Engineer passionate about merging industrial automation with cutting-edge AI. With hands-on experience in **PLC programming, SCADA systems, and HMI design**, I build reliable and intelligent automation solutions for industrial environments.

From designing circuits in **AutoCAD** to deploying neural networks on **ESP32 microcontrollers**, I bridge the gap between the physical and digital worlds.
